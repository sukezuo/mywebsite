Biography

It's youyang' biography. Ningbo Univercity

I am currently an assistant professor at The University of Hong Kong. My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and medical image analysis. I am dedicated to designing data-efficient learning methods for biomedical image analysis and reconstruction. I also have expertise in deep learning for 3D vision.Before joining HKU, I was a postdoctoral research fellow in Stanford University, working with Prof. Lei Xing. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in CSE, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng and Prof. Chi-Wing Fu in July 2019. Before that, I received the B.Eng degree in CS, Zhejiang University in 2015, under the supervision of Prof. Deng Cai.

I am looking for self-motivated PhD students, postdocs, research assistants, and (remote) summer interns, who are interested in artificial intelligence for healthcare. Welcome to drop me an email with your CV and transcripts. Remote collaboration is also welcome! If you are an HKU student interested in doing research with me, please send me an email.
